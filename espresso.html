Espresso Coffee

Experience the bold and concentrated flavors of espresso. Here, we explore the art of crafting the perfect espresso shot and its variations. Push water through coffee with a pressure of 9 BAR, and TADA, Espresso.

Espresso Varieties

Name Description Caffeine Content
Single Espresso A single shot of espresso, extracted with finely ground coffee beans. Approximately 63 mg
Double Espresso Double the delight with a double shot of espresso, perfect for a stronger flavor. Approximately 126 mg
Americano Espresso shots diluted with hot water, providing a milder taste. Varies (depends on ratio)
Cappuccino Espresso shots mixed with steamed milk and topped with foamed milk. Varies (Depends on ratio)
